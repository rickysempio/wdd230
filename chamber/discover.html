Enchnated Kingdom is the first and only world-class theme park in the country. The theme park offers thrilling rides and kind-of Disneyland attractions. Ayala Malls

Where the sun meets the wind, Solenad is a pedestrian-friendly shopping and dining destination that celebrates the outdoors. Set amidst the natural environs of the NUVALI estate, it is a sprawling landscape of green and open spaces where outdoor recreational activities take place.

Nuvali Park

NUVALI is the country's first and largest eco-city development, built on the principles of sustainable design. A project of Ayala Land, NUVALI is a 2,500-hectare mixed-use development that straddles the cities of Sta. Rosa, Cabuyao and Calamba in Laguna.

Museo De Santa Rosa

The Santa Rosa Museum building was built in 1828 during the Spanish period as a government Building under the reign of Gobernadorcillo Don Bernardo Zavalla, and in 1954 the building was re-organized. he former town hall now showcases the rich history of Sta. Rosa, and its significance to the whole of Laguna.
